Central Capital Finance (CCF) - Self-Service Account Opening Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: July 2026

Please read these Terms and Conditions carefully before initiating your account application. By checking the "I accept" box and proceeding with the self-service registration, you agree to be legally bound by this agreement, our Privacy Policy, and all relevant Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe (RBZ) regulations.

1. Scope & Eligibility

  • You must be at least 18 years of age to open a self-service personal account.
  • Applications are restricted to Zimbabwean citizens, permanent residents, or foreigners with valid, verified residence permits.
  • You warrant that all information provided during this application is true, accurate, current, and complete.

2. KYC (Know Your Customer) & Digital Identity Verification

Under the guidelines of the Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe (RBZ), CCF is required to verify the identity of all account holders. You hereby consent and authorize Central Capital Finance to:

  • Cross-reference your National ID or Passport details with official governmental registrar databases.
  • Verify your residential address using utility bills, lease agreements, or other approved documents.
  • Perform real-time facial biometric and liveness checks via your device camera to prevent identity theft.

3. Anti-Money Laundering (AML) & Sanctions Compliance

  • You agree that the account will not be used for any illicit activities, including money laundering, terrorist financing, tax evasion, or trade in restricted assets.
  • CCF reserves the right to reject, suspend, or terminate any application or account immediately if a sanctions screening check returns a positive hit or if your profile fails compliance vetting.

4. US Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act (FATCA) Declaration

If you are a US Citizen, US Permanent Resident (Green Card Holder), or have a US tax status, you are legally required to declare this. Failure to declare or provide the necessary IRS tax forms (e.g., W-9) will result in immediate disqualification and reporting under international tax treaties.

5. Data Privacy & Consent

Pursuant to the Cyber and Data Protection Act [Chapter 12:07] of Zimbabwe, you consent to Central Capital Finance collecting, processing, and storing your sensitive personal data (including biological photos, signatures, address documents, and ID particulars) exclusively for account administration, regulatory compliance, and risk management.

6. Electronic Communications & Notifications

By signing this agreement digitally, you agree to receive automated verification codes (OTP), transaction notifications, and administrative updates via SMS to your registered mobile number and/or email address.

7. Electronic Execution & Consent

You acknowledge that checking the "I accept the Terms and Conditions" checkbox constitutes your digital signature, carrying the same weight and legal enforceability as a physical handwritten signature.
